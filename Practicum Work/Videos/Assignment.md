# Post Video # and Submit Peer Feedback Report

Every other week you should post to the corresponding discussion board a ~3-5 minute video update on your progress with the Capstone project. This video is an opportunity to:

1. Summarize some of your activities from the past week
2. Discuss ideas you have for the week ahead and
3. Finish your video by highlighting areas where you're looking for feedback.

The video can be a simple recording of you talking to the camera or a voice over of some slides submitted with "Record/Upload Media."  Additional information on posting video content to Canvas will be shared in Ed Discussion.

In the week following the posting of your video, watch the videos of others in your Group to see where your peers are making progress, what kinds of challenges they are facing, and what tools and techniques they're employing. You'll be expected to provide feedback with brief but thoughtful replies, e.g., suggesting additional resources or related ideas and how they might be helpful to the student's project, constructive criticism on how concepts or explanations are delivered, etc. While you should watch every video, you do not need to comment on each one. Given 8-10 videos of ~3-5 minutes each, and typing up your responsive thoughts, we expect this activity should take you a little less than an hour every other week. Discussion will be closed the following week and you'll be graded for the quality of engagement.

Your weekly update video should be posted by the listed due date. Then your group will leave feedback after that date during the following week.
