## 🧾 IRB & Human Subjects Guidance

- IRB approval depends on what data you collect:
  - Likely needed if using personal or identifiable data
  - Often not needed for:
    - Policy research
    - General stakeholder opinions
- Best practices:
  - Provide informed consent
  - Keep responses confidential and anonymized
  - Optionally let participants review results (with time limits)

## 📊 Data & Research Decisions

👉 Show your reasoning process, even if you pivot.
